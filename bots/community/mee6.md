## 🎯 What This Bot Does

MEE6 is one of the most widely known Discord bots and serves as an all-in-one solution for community management.

It combines moderation, leveling systems, role rewards, announcements, social media integrations, and automation into a single platform. Its dashboard-first design allows server owners to configure most features without touching commands.

MEE6 is frequently used by gaming communities, creators, streamers, and public servers due to its ease of use and broad feature set.
