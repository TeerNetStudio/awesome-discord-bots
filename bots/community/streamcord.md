## 🎯 What This Bot Does

Streamcord helps Discord communities stay connected with content creators by automatically announcing new streams, videos, uploads, and live events.

Instead of manually posting notifications whenever a creator goes live, Streamcord handles the process automatically and can send rich embedded messages to designated channels.

It is especially popular among streamers, content creators, esports organizations, gaming communities, and creator-focused Discord servers.
