## 🎯 What This Bot Does

Lurkr is designed to improve community engagement through activity tracking, achievement systems, economy mechanics, and progression rewards.

Unlike traditional leveling bots that focus mainly on XP, Lurkr introduces quests, achievements, collectibles, and economy-based progression systems that encourage long-term participation.

The bot is especially popular in gaming, anime, and social communities where member activity is a major priority.
