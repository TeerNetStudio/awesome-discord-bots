## 🎯 What This Bot Does

Tatsu is one of Discord's most established community engagement bots. It focuses on member retention through profile systems, leveling mechanics, reputation features, virtual currency, collectibles, and social interactions.

Unlike moderation-focused bots, Tatsu aims to create long-term engagement by rewarding activity and giving members progression systems to interact with.

Many communities use Tatsu alongside moderation bots such as Carl-bot or Dyno.
