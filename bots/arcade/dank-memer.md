## 🎯 What This Bot Does

Dank Memer is one of the largest and most recognizable Discord bots, focused on entertainment, virtual economies, collectibles, and social interaction.

Originally known for meme-related commands, the bot has evolved into a massive ecosystem featuring currency systems, item trading, pets, adventures, crime mechanics, gambling games, achievements, and seasonal events.

Many communities use Dank Memer to increase engagement and provide long-term progression systems for members.
