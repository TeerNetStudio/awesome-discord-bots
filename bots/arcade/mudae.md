## 🎯 What This Bot Does

Mudae is one of the largest anime and gaming bots available on Discord.

The bot allows users to collect characters from anime, manga, video games, visual novels, and other media franchises. Members can claim characters, build collections, trade with other users, create wishlists, and compete for rare characters.

Its extensive database and long-term progression systems have made it one of the most active entertainment bots in the Discord ecosystem.

Many anime communities build entire server events and economies around Mudae.
