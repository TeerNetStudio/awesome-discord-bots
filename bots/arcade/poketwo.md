## 🎯 What This Bot Does

Pokétwo brings a Pokémon-inspired collecting experience to Discord servers.

Pokémon spawn naturally while members chat, allowing users to catch, train, trade, and collect hundreds of different Pokémon directly within Discord.

The bot features progression systems, trading mechanics, battles, achievements, and Pokédex completion goals that encourage long-term engagement.

Pokétwo is particularly popular among gaming communities, anime communities, and public Discord servers.
