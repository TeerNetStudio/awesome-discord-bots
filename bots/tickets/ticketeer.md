## 🎯 What This Bot Does

Ticketeer extends traditional Discord ticket systems by combining support tickets with forms, questionnaires, and application workflows.

Instead of simply opening a support channel, Ticketeer can collect structured information before creating the ticket. This makes it useful for staff applications, partnership requests, whitelist applications, bug reports, and onboarding processes.

Communities that need more than simple support tickets often choose Ticketeer to streamline information gathering.
