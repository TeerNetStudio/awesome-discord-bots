## 🎯 What This Bot Does

VoiceMaster helps Discord communities manage voice channels through automated Join-to-Create systems.

Instead of maintaining large numbers of permanent voice channels, members join a designated creation channel and automatically receive their own temporary voice room.

Channel owners can customize permissions, user limits, names, and privacy settings without requiring moderator assistance.

VoiceMaster is particularly popular in gaming communities, creator servers, and social hubs where voice activity is frequent.
