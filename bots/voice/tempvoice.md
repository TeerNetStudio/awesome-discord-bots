## 🎯 What This Bot Does

TempVoice automates the creation and management of voice channels.

Instead of maintaining dozens of permanent voice channels that remain empty most of the time, TempVoice allows users to join a designated channel which automatically creates a temporary voice room. When the room becomes empty, it is automatically deleted.

This helps keep servers organized while providing members with private voice spaces whenever needed.
