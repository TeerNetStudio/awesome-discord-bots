## 🎯 What This Bot Does

Hydra is one of Discord's most popular music bots, designed to provide a clean and user-friendly music experience.

The bot focuses on ease of use, playlist management, music discovery, and high-quality playback. Its dashboard allows administrators and users to manage settings without relying entirely on commands.

Hydra is often recommended for communities that want a modern music bot with a professional interface and straightforward setup process.
