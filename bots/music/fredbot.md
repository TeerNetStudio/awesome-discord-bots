## 🎯 What This Bot Does

FredBoat is one of Discord's most well-known music bots and is particularly respected for its reliability, open-source nature, and self-hosting support.

The bot allows users to play music from supported sources, manage playlists, queue tracks, and control playback through simple commands. Unlike many music bots that rely entirely on hosted services, FredBoat also offers self-hosting options for communities that want complete control over uptime and configuration.

Its stability and transparency have made it a popular choice among music-focused communities for many years.
