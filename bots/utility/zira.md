## 🎯 What This Bot Does

Zira is one of Discord's most well-known reaction role bots.

The bot allows server administrators to create self-assignable role menus using reactions, enabling members to choose interests, notifications, game preferences, region roles, pronouns, and other server-specific roles without staff involvement.

Its simplicity and reliability have made it a popular choice for community onboarding and role management across a wide variety of Discord servers.
