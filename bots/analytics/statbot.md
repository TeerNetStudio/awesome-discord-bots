## 🎯 What This Bot Does

Statbot helps Discord administrators understand how their communities are performing through detailed statistics and activity tracking.

The bot collects data about messages, member activity, channel engagement, server growth, and participation trends. These insights allow community managers to make informed decisions and identify areas for improvement.

Unlike moderation bots, Statbot focuses entirely on measurement and reporting.
