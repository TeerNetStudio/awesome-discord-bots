## ЁЯОп What This Bot Does

Logger is a specialized Discord bot built specifically for logging and server event tracking.

Unlike moderation bots that include logging as a secondary feature, Logger focuses almost entirely on recording actions occurring within a server. This includes message edits, deletions, role changes, moderation actions, joins, leaves, voice activity, and more.

The bot is commonly used by moderation teams that require detailed records and evidence for investigations and appeals.
