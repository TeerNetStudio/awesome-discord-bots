## ЁЯОп What This Bot Does

ProBot is one of the largest all-in-one Discord bots, combining moderation, automod, welcome systems, leveling, logging, and community engagement features.

Unlike bots that focus primarily on moderation, ProBot places significant emphasis on member engagement. Features such as leveling, welcome cards, reputation systems, and customizable messages help communities grow while still maintaining moderation capabilities.

Its simple setup process makes it especially attractive to newer server owners.
