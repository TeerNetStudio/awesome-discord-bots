## 🎯 What This Bot Does

Sapphire is a modern moderation bot designed to provide many of the features found in established bots like Carl-bot and Dyno while offering a cleaner and more intuitive dashboard experience.

The bot focuses on moderation, logging, automod, utility features, ticketing integrations, reaction roles, and server automation. It is often recommended to administrators looking for a more modern alternative to older moderation bots.

Sapphire aims to reduce setup complexity while still offering extensive customization options.
