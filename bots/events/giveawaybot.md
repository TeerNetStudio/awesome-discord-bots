## 🎯 What This Bot Does

GiveawayBot helps Discord communities run giveaways, contests, raffles, and promotional events.

The bot automates winner selection, rerolls, eligibility requirements, role restrictions, and giveaway scheduling. This removes the need for manual management while ensuring fairness and transparency.

It is commonly used by gaming communities, creators, brands, and public servers to boost engagement and reward members.
