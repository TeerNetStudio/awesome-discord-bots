## 🎯 What This Bot Does

CommunityOne is a community management platform that extends Discord with forms, applications, onboarding systems, memberships, and engagement tools.

The platform helps communities collect structured information from members, automate onboarding processes, manage applications, and organize community operations through a centralized dashboard.

It is commonly used by creator communities, gaming organizations, educational groups, startup communities, and membership-based servers.
