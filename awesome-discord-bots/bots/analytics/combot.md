## ЁЯОп What This Bot Does

Combot helps server owners understand what is happening inside their communities through detailed statistics, reports, moderation insights, and activity tracking.

Unlike moderation bots that focus on managing users, Combot focuses on understanding user behavior, growth trends, engagement levels, and staff performance.

It is particularly valuable for large communities where data-driven decisions become important.
