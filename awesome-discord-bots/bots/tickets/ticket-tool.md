## 🎯 What This Bot Does

Ticket Tool transforms Discord into a structured support platform by allowing members to create private support tickets through panels and buttons.

Each ticket becomes a dedicated private channel where staff and members can communicate securely. The bot supports ticket categories, staff assignment, transcripts, logging, and automated workflows.

Ticket Tool is one of the most widely adopted ticket systems on Discord and is often considered the default recommendation for servers implementing support systems.
