## 🎯 What This Bot Does

Beemo is a Discord security bot designed to help communities identify suspicious accounts, prevent raids, and reduce abuse from alternate accounts.

The bot focuses on member verification, risk assessment, and server protection rather than traditional moderation. It helps administrators evaluate incoming members before they gain access to the server.

Many communities use Beemo alongside moderation bots such as Carl-bot and security solutions such as Wick.
