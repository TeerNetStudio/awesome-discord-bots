## ЁЯОп What This Bot Does

Captcha.bot specializes in one task: ensuring that new members are human before they gain access to your server.

Unlike broader security bots such as Wick, Captcha.bot focuses on verification and access control. It places a verification step between joining and participation, making it significantly harder for automated accounts and raid bots to enter the server.

Many communities use Captcha.bot as a first line of defense alongside moderation and security bots.
