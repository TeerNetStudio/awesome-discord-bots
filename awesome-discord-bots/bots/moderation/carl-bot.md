## 🎯 What This Bot Does

Carl-bot is one of the most widely used Discord moderation bots. It combines moderation tools, reaction roles, logging, automod, welcome systems, and custom commands into a single platform.

The bot is particularly known for its reaction role system and highly configurable logging module. Many communities use Carl-bot as their primary moderation and server management bot.

It is suitable for both small communities and large public servers.
