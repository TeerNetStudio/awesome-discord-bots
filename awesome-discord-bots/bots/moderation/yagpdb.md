## 🎯 What This Bot Does

YAGPDB (Yet Another General Purpose Discord Bot) is one of the most powerful moderation and automation bots available for Discord.

Unlike many moderation bots that focus on ease of use, YAGPDB focuses on flexibility. It provides moderation tools, reaction roles, automod, logging, and one of the most advanced custom command systems available on Discord.

The bot is especially popular among power users, developers, and server administrators who want to build custom workflows without creating their own Discord bot from scratch.
