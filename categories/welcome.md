## 📌 Category Overview

Bots in this category primarily focus on:

- Welcome messages
- Member onboarding
- Auto-role assignment
- Community introductions
- Join and leave notifications

### Inclusion Criteria

A bot belongs in this category if it:

- Primarily improves onboarding experiences
- Provides welcome or greeting functionality
- Is publicly available
- Is actively maintained

### Exclusions

The following do not belong in this category:

- Verification-only bots
- Moderation-only bots
- Utility bots with minimal welcome features
- Abandoned projects

## 🎯 Choosing the Right Bot

### Decision Matrix

| If your priority is... | Consider |

| Best customization | Carl-bot |
| Beautiful welcome images | MEE6 |

### Key Questions

**Do you need advanced automation?**
→ Carl-bot.

**Do you want attractive welcome graphics?**
→ MEE6 or ProBot.

→ Welcomer.
