# 🤖 AI Bots

> Bots focused on artificial intelligence, conversational assistants, content generation, moderation assistance, productivity, and AI-powered community experiences.

**Navigation:** [Main Directory](../README.md) · [All Categories](../README.md#-categories)

AI bots bring artificial intelligence capabilities into Discord servers. They can answer questions, generate content, summarize conversations, assist moderators, automate support, and provide interactive experiences for communities.

This category covers AI assistants, chatbot platforms, generative AI tools, AI moderation assistants, and productivity-focused AI integrations.

> ⚠️ AI capabilities evolve rapidly. Features, models, pricing, and limitations may change frequently. Always verify current capabilities on official websites.

## 📌 Category Overview

### What this category covers

Bots in this category provide one or more of the following:

* AI conversations
* Question answering
* Content generation
* Summarization
* AI moderation assistance
* Productivity tools
* Knowledge retrieval
* Image generation integrations
* Server assistance
* Community support automation

### What qualifies for inclusion

A bot belongs in this category if it:

* Primarily focuses on AI-powered functionality
* Uses machine learning or language models
* Is actively maintained
* Offers meaningful AI features to Discord users

### What does NOT qualify

The following do not belong in this category:

* Standard chatbot bots without AI
* Utility bots with basic responses
* Abandoned AI projects
* Non-public AI integrations
* Simple command-based bots
