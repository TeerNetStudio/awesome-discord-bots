# 🎙️ Voice Bots

> Bots focused on voice channel management, temporary voice channels, Join-to-Create systems, voice moderation, and voice community experiences.

**Navigation:** [Main Directory](../README.md) · [All Categories](../README.md#-categories)

Voice bots help communities organize and improve Discord voice channel experiences. Instead of managing static voice channels manually, these bots automate creation, permissions, ownership, moderation, and customization of voice spaces.

This category covers Join-to-Create systems, temporary voice channels, voice management utilities, voice moderation, and voice automation solutions.

## 📌 Category Overview

### What this category covers

Bots in this category provide one or more of the following:

* Join-to-Create voice channels
* Temporary voice channels
* Voice ownership controls
* Voice moderation
* Voice automation
* Channel management
* Voice permissions
* Voice activity systems
* Channel customization
* Voice community tools

### What qualifies for inclusion

A bot belongs in this category if it:

* Primarily focuses on voice channel management
* Improves Discord voice experiences
* Is actively maintained
* Provides automation beyond Discord's built-in voice features

### What does NOT qualify

The following do not belong in this category:

* Music bots
* General moderation bots
* Utility bots with minor voice features
* Abandoned voice projects
* Private voice management systems

## 🤖 Bots in This Category

### [TempVoice](../bots/voice/tempvoice.md)

One of the most popular Join-to-Create bots available. Automatically creates temporary voice channels and removes them when no longer needed.

**Best for:** General voice management.
**Free tier:** Generous.
**Dashboard:** Yes.
**Open source:** No.

---

### [VoiceMaster](../bots/voice/voicemaster.md)

A voice-focused bot that provides advanced channel ownership tools, permissions management, customization options, and automation features.

**Best for:** Advanced voice communities.
**Free tier:** Available.
**Dashboard:** Yes.
**Open source:** No.

---

### [Auto Voice Channels](../bots/voice/auto-voice-channels.md)

A lightweight Join-to-Create solution focused on automatic channel creation and cleanup.

**Best for:** Simplicity and lightweight setups.
**Free tier:** Available.
**Dashboard:** Limited.
**Open source:** No.

## 🎯 Choosing the Right Bot

| If your priority is...   | Consider            |
| ------------------------ | ------------------- |
| Easiest setup            | TempVoice           |
| Advanced controls        | VoiceMaster         |
| Lightweight solution     | Auto Voice Channels |
| Large public server      | TempVoice           |
| Custom voice communities | VoiceMaster         |
| Automatic cleanup        | TempVoice           |

### Key Questions

**Do you need temporary channels?**
→ TempVoice.

**Do users want control over their voice rooms?**
→ VoiceMaster.

**Do you want a simple Join-to-Create system?**
→ Auto Voice Channels.

**Do you manage many active voice channels?**
→ TempVoice.

## 📝 Contributor Notes

### Adding a Voice Bot

* Voice management must be the primary feature.
* Verify Join-to-Create functionality.
* Confirm active maintenance.

### Category-Specific Notes

* Voice bots are often confused with music bots.
* Only include bots focused on voice channel management.
* Recheck dashboard and customization capabilities periodically.
