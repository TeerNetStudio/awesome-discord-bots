# 💰 Economy Bots

> Bots focused on virtual currencies, banking systems, shops, trading, gambling mechanics, collectibles, and progression-based economies.

**Navigation:** [Main Directory](../README.md) · [All Categories](../README.md#-categories)

Economy bots introduce virtual financial systems into Discord communities. They allow members to earn currency, purchase items, trade collectibles, compete through wealth rankings, and participate in community-driven economies.

This category covers currency systems, virtual shops, inventory management, trading systems, collectible economies, and economy-based progression mechanics.

## 📌 Category Overview

### What this category covers

Bots in this category provide one or more of the following:

* Virtual currencies
* Banking systems
* Item shops
* Trading systems
* Inventories
* Collectibles
* Daily rewards
* Wealth leaderboards
* Jobs and earning systems
* Economy progression

### What qualifies for inclusion

A bot belongs in this category if it:

* Primarily focuses on economy mechanics
* Provides a persistent currency system
* Encourages engagement through rewards
* Is actively maintained

### What does NOT qualify

The following do not belong in this category:

* Leveling-only bots
* Moderation bots
* Analytics bots
* Utility bots with simple point systems
* Abandoned economy projects

## 🤖 Bots in This Category

### [Mudae](../bots/economy/mudae.md)

One of Discord's largest collection-based economy bots. Members collect characters, manage inventories, and participate in a long-term progression system.

**Best for:** Anime and collection communities.
**Free tier:** Available.
**Dashboard:** No.
**Open source:** No.

---

### [OwO](../bots/economy/owo.md)

A popular economy and collectible bot featuring hunting, battles, pets, inventories, and progression systems.

**Best for:** Gamified communities.
**Free tier:** Available.
**Dashboard:** Limited.
**Open source:** No.

### [UnbelievaBoat](../bots/economy/unbelievaboat.md)

One of Discord's most established economy bots, offering currencies, banking, shops, custom rewards, and server-specific economies.

**Best for:** Traditional economy systems.
**Free tier:** Generous.
**Dashboard:** Yes.
**Open source:** No.

An RPG-focused economy bot featuring quests, progression, classes, and virtual economies.

**Best for:** RPG communities.
**Free tier:** Available.
