## 📌 Category Overview

Bots in this category primarily focus on:

- Giveaway creation and management
- Automated winner selection
- Entry requirement verification
- Community engagement campaigns

### Inclusion Criteria

A bot belongs in this category if it:

- Primarily serves giveaway management
- Is publicly available
- Is actively maintained
- Supports automated winner selection

### Exclusions

The following do not belong in this category:

- General moderation bots with basic giveaway commands
- Utility bots where giveaways are secondary
- Abandoned projects
- Private giveaway systems
