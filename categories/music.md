# 🎵 Music Bots

> Bots focused on music playback, audio streaming, playlists, radio stations, and shared listening experiences in Discord voice channels.

**Navigation:** [Main Directory](../README.md) · [All Categories](../README.md#-categories)

Music bots allow communities to listen to music together inside Discord voice channels. They provide features such as queues, playlists, audio filters, equalizers, radio streaming, and 24/7 playback.

This category covers dedicated music bots that prioritize audio playback and listening experiences. Due to frequent platform and API changes, music bot capabilities may evolve over time. Always verify supported sources before deploying a music bot.

> ⚠️ Music bot features can change rapidly due to third-party platform restrictions. Verify source compatibility on the bot's official website before deployment.

## 📌 Category Overview

### What this category covers

Bots in this category provide one or more of the following:

* Music playback
* Playlist management
* Audio queues
* Radio streaming
* Audio filters
* Equalizers
* Voice channel music
* Shared listening sessions
* 24/7 playback
* Multi-source audio support

### What qualifies for inclusion

A bot belongs in this category if it:

* Primarily focuses on music or audio playback
* Is publicly available
* Is actively maintained
* Supports Discord voice channels

### What does NOT qualify

The following do not belong in this category:

* General utility bots with basic music commands
* Soundboard-only bots
* Voice management bots
* Abandoned music projects
* Private music bots

## 🎯 Choosing the Right Bot

| If your priority is...  | Consider     |
| ----------------------- | ------------ |
| Free music playback     | FredBoat     |
| Dashboard controls      | Hydra        |
| Audio filters           | Lava Music   |
| Multiple voice channels | Jockie Music |
| Continuous streaming    | 24/7         |
| Easy setup              | Chip         |

### Key Questions

**Do you need multiple music channels simultaneously?**
→ Jockie Music.

**Do you prefer browser-based controls?**
→ Hydra.

**Do audio effects matter?**
→ Lava Music or Uzox.

**Do you want a free and proven solution?**
→ FredBoat.
