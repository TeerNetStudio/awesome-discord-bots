# 🎮 Arcade Bots

> Bots focused on games, collectibles, RPG systems, mini-games, progression mechanics, and entertainment-driven community engagement.

**Navigation:** [Main Directory](../README.md) · [All Categories](../README.md#-categories)

Arcade bots transform Discord servers into interactive gaming communities. They provide mini-games, RPG systems, collectible mechanics, battles, quests, achievements, and long-term progression systems that keep members engaged beyond regular conversation.

This category covers game-focused bots, collectible systems, RPG experiences, idle games, trivia bots, and entertainment platforms designed primarily for fun and engagement.

## 📌 Category Overview

### What this category covers

Bots in this category provide one or more of the following:

* RPG systems
* Collectible mechanics
* Character progression
* Mini-games
* Quests and adventures
* Pet systems
* Trading
* Battles and PvP
* Trivia games
* Community competitions

### What qualifies for inclusion

A bot belongs in this category if it:

* Primarily focuses on gaming or entertainment
* Provides interactive gameplay
* Encourages long-term engagement
* Is actively maintained

### What does NOT qualify

The following do not belong in this category:

* Economy-only bots
* Utility bots
* Community bots with minor games
* Abandoned gaming projects
* Single-purpose joke commands
