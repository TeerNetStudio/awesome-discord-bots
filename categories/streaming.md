## 📌 Category Overview

Bots in this category primarily focus on:

- Stream notifications
- YouTube upload alerts
- Twitch integration
- Creator community engagement
- Livestream scheduling

### Inclusion Criteria

A bot belongs in this category if it:

- Primarily focuses on streaming platforms
- Provides creator or livestream integrations
- Is publicly available
- Is actively maintained

### Exclusions

The following do not belong in this category:

- General utility bots
- Community bots with minor notification features
- Abandoned projects
- Platform-specific tools unavailable to the public

## 🎯 Choosing the Right Bot

### Decision Matrix

| If your priority is... | Consider |
|------------------------|---------|
| Twitch notifications | Streamcord |
| YouTube uploads | MonitoRSS |
| Creator community | Streamcord |
| Multi-platform support | Notifier |
| Simplicity | LiveBot |

### Key Questions

**Do you stream on multiple platforms?**
→ Notifier or Streamcord.

**Do you need YouTube upload notifications?**
→ MonitoRSS.

**Do you primarily stream on Twitch?**
→ Streamcord or TwitchBot.
