# 📝 Forms Bots

> Bots focused on applications, surveys, questionnaires, onboarding forms, feedback collection, and structured data gathering.

**Navigation:** [Main Directory](../README.md) · [All Categories](../README.md#-categories)

Forms bots help Discord communities collect information in a structured and organized manner. They replace messy message-based submissions with guided forms, questionnaires, surveys, and application systems.

This category covers application bots, survey systems, onboarding forms, feedback collection tools, and structured submission platforms.

## 📌 Category Overview

### What this category covers

Bots in this category provide one or more of the following:

* Staff applications
* Partnership applications
* Feedback forms
* Surveys and polls
* User onboarding forms
* Event registrations
* Bug report forms
* Structured submissions
* Recruitment systems
* Custom questionnaires

### What qualifies for inclusion

A bot belongs in this category if it:

* Primarily focuses on forms and structured submissions
* Supports custom questions or workflows
* Is actively maintained
* Improves information collection

### What does NOT qualify

The following do not belong in this category:

* Ticket-only bots
* Poll-only bots
* Utility bots with basic forms
* Abandoned projects
* Private enterprise systems

## 🤖 Bots in This Category

### [Application Bot](../bots/forms/application-bot.md)

A forms platform focused on recruitment, staff applications, partnerships, and structured review workflows.

**Best for:** Recruitment and applications.
**Free tier:** Available.
**Dashboard:** Yes.
**Open source:** No.

---

### [Formify](../bots/forms/formify.md)

A flexible form builder designed for surveys, feedback collection, questionnaires, and general submissions.

**Best for:** Community feedback.
**Free tier:** Available.
**Dashboard:** Yes.
**Open source:** No.

---

### [Ticketeer](../bots/tickets/ticketeer.md)

Combines forms and tickets into a unified workflow, allowing communities to collect information before creating support channels.

**Best for:** Applications and support intake.
**Free tier:** Available.
**Dashboard:** Yes.
**Open source:** No.
