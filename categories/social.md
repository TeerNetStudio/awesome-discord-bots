## 📌 Category Overview

Bots in this category primarily focus on:

- User profiles
- Reputation systems
- Social interactions
- Community relationships
- Member recognition

### Inclusion Criteria

A bot belongs in this category if it:

- Primarily encourages social interaction
- Provides profiles or relationship features
- Supports community engagement
- Is actively maintained

### Exclusions

The following do not belong in this category:

- Leveling-first bots
- Economy-first bots
- Utility bots
- Abandoned projects

## 🎯 Choosing the Right Bot

### Decision Matrix

| If your priority is... | Consider |
|------------------------|---------|
| Community engagement | Tatsu |
| Profile customization | Mimu |
| Large communities | MEE6 |
| Casual interactions | OwO |
| Relationship systems | Aiko |

### Key Questions

**Do you want customizable profiles?**
→ Mimu or Tatsu.

**Do you want reputation systems?**
→ Tatsu.

**Do you want lightweight social features?**
→ OwO.
